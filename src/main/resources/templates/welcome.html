Welcome to Gastro Tracker

Gastro Tracker is your personalized tool for managing and monitoring dietary habits. Whether you're a patient or a doctor, this application aims to simplify the process of tracking and maintaining a healthy lifestyle for your digestive system.

For Patients:

  • Effortlessly log your daily meals, including breakfast, lunch, and dinner.
  • Monitor meal times and note any discomfort or symptoms experienced.
  • Easily search and filter through your meal history.
  • Schedule and manage appointments directly within the app.
  • Track ongoing treatments and medications.
  • Collaborate with your healthcare provider by sharing meal data for more informed discussions during appointments.

For Doctors:

  • Gain valuable insights into patients' eating patterns and symptoms.
  • Review detailed meal histories to better understand correlations with gastrointestinal health.
  • Provide personalized recommendations based on recorded data.
  • Enhance communication and engagement with patients for improved medical outcomes.
